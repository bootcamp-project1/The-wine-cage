About Snackolas

This website was built to give our users, not only an application to pair a good movie with a good snack, but also give them a good chuckle when they realize they will only be suggested a Nicolas Cage movie.


Thanks to Spoonacular and The Movie Database we are able to provide our guests a Nicolas Cage movie that still aligns with their search categories. Whether it be action, comedy, or romance, Cage has got it all and we're ready to provide you with an excellent choice and a great snack to go along with it.
