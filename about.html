About The Wine Cage


This website was built to give our users not only an application to pair a good movie with a good wine, but also to give them a good chuckle when they realize they will only be suggested a Nicolas Cage movie.


Thanks to Spoonacular and The Movie Database we are able to provide our guests with a Nicolas Cage movie that aligns with their search categories. Whether the user is craving action, comedy, or romance, Cage has got it all! We are ready to deliver an excellent movie choice and a great wine to go along with it.


You can check out our repository on github atThe Wine Cage.
